Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 20, 2026

ClearNotify ("we", "us", or "our") provides a notification service that sends transactional SMS messages to users who have opted in. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information.

Information we collect

  • Phone number: Collected when a user provides a number to receive account alerts or verification codes.
  • Message metadata: Limited delivery-related data (e.g., timestamps and delivery status) may be processed to operate the service.

How we use information

  • Transactional messaging: To send verification codes (OTP), password reset messages, and important account-related alerts.
  • Service operations: To troubleshoot delivery issues and maintain reliability.

What we do not do

  • No marketing: We do not send promotional or marketing SMS messages.
  • No sale of data: We do not sell personal information.

Sharing

We may use service providers (such as messaging carriers and communications platforms) to deliver messages. We share only what is necessary to provide the service.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Opt-out

Users can opt out at any time by replying STOP.
